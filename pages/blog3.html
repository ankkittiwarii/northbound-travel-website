Experience the Life of Kings

Rajasthan is home to ancient palaces that have been transformed into world-class luxury hotels. Northbound brings you the top 3 picks for an ultimate royal stay.

Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur

Floating in the middle of Lake Pichola, this white marble palace is the pinnacle of romance and luxury.

Umaid Bhawan, Jodhpur

One of the world's largest private residences, offering a grand look at the lifestyle of the Marwar royalty.
